My PC

It consists of these parts I built it myself

  • Corsair Rm750
  • Asus Rog Strix B550
  • AMD Ryzen 5 5600x
  • Samsung 1TB 970EVO
  • Corsair 32GB
  • NZXT Kraken M22 Liquid Cooler
  • Nvidia 3060ti

PC origin story

I built it in 2021 during covid. The inspiration came from my friends as they all somehow seemed to have built PC's. I went to the Micro Center in Irvine and bought everything the only thing that was hard to get was the graphics card because people were buying them to mine bitcoin.

PC capability

Have the power to run any game with high on all the settings turned on such as ARK for example. It works great for multitasking as I can open tabs and have apps running in the back easy with no slowing down. I used to have a really old computer that would take a minute or two to boot up and now its up and seconds I love it.
